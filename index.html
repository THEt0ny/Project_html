Tribute to Paul Walker

His brief biography

Paul William Walker IV commonly known as just Paul Walker, is a male actor born on September 12, 1973 known for his iconic role as Brian O'conner in the Fast and Furious franchise. He is the father of Meadow Walker his daughter and brother to Cody and Caleb Walker. His father Paul William Walker III and his mother who is Cheryl Walker. Paul died on November 30, 2013 in a car crash.


His career

He started at a very young age. His very first tv appearence was in a pampers commercial when he was only 2 years old. He kept doing tv commercial before starting his acting career in 1984 in a teen anthology series CBS Schoolbreak Special. Paul kept working in television until 1996, across all genres. His film career started in 1986 in low budgets B films. His first was in a horror comedy Monster in the Closet. After his movie 'The Skulls' (2000s). He then got his breakthrough role starring the popular action movie, The Fast and the Furious along side Vin diesel. He was nominated for the MTV Movie awrad for best on-screen team with Vin diesel in 2002 for the movie. In 2002, he starred in a critically successful film called Joy Ride. He then went on to starred in low budget films or commercially unsuccessful films like Timeline(2003) and Into the Blue(2005). Walker went on to starred in the second movie of the fast and furious franchise 2 fast 2 furious and the 4,5 and 6 movie of the franchise. He was able to film only a part of the 7 movie before his passing. The film makers were able to use his brothers and special effects to recreate a perfect image of him to then release the film in 2017 after pushing to the release date back.

The franchise has 9 movies and 1 spin-off movie. 2 new movies are coming soon too to make a total of 11 movies and 1 spin-off.

His hobbies

Paul really didn't have much hobbies but the two that he had was cars and marine life (everything ocean related). Just to remind you Paul studied in marine biology before the start of his acting career and in his free time he would fund marine life foundations millions and even created one to his name. Paul spent his time to help raise money for charities too. His other hobbie for cars isn't a surprise. He holds a massive car collection shared with his dear friend Roger Rodas. The unexpected

As we all know by now Paul life didn't end on a good note. His life ended after his friend Roger Rodas who was behind the wheel of Paul's red Porsche Carrera GT 2005. Lost control of the car and hit a parking tree at about 100 miles an hour. Paul and Rodas perished in the burning car on November 30, 2012.
